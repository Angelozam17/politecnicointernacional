Ingles Markets, Inc. is an American regional supermarket chain based in Black Mountain, North Carolina.[6] As of January 2018, the company operates 212 supermarkets in the Southeastern United States. The company, although listed on the NASDAQ, is family-controlled, with 86% of the voting power and nearly half of the shares owned by the Ingle family.

As an adjunct to its supermarket business, Ingles owns and operates shopping centers, gas stations and a milk processing and packaging plant.
